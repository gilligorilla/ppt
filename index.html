ABOUT

PPT is a foundation solutions contractor.

Precast Piling Technology, Inc. (PPT) was established in 2009 with a mission to revitalize the precast piling market in Florida. We used precast piles as our tool to optimize constructability, schedule, and efficiency of foundation design and construction. We found success using our in-house engineering and manufacturing to increase pile capacities, reduce sizes, lengths, and quantities, and redesign connections and caps to reduce costs and reduce days on site.

Our mission at PPT has since evolved to foundation system optimization, including projects with other pile types. Following our value-driven approach, we will design and install the foundation system best-suited for the subsurface and project demands, whether that is precast piles, pipe piles, h-piles, timber piles, and augercast piles.
